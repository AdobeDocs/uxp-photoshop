| green | *number* | R W | 255 | 0..255 | 23.0 | The green color value. |
| hexValue | *string* | R W | - | - | 23.0 | The hexadecimal representation of the color in uppercase. |
| red | *number* | R W | 255 | 0..255 | 23.0 | The red color value. |
